Who We Are

At House of Humanity, we are driven by a purpose to uplift lives with compassion and dignity. What began as a simple act during crisis has grown into a structured movement impacting thousands across India. Our strength lies in our volunteers, our mission in empathy, and our vision in sustainable transformation.

We believe in empowering communities—not just helping them. From education to healthcare, hunger to hygiene, every initiative we take stems from one belief: humanity comes first. Guided by empathy and fueled by collective action, we transform everyday kindness into long-term impact for communities in need.

Our Impact Initiatives

At House of Humanity, our projects are rooted in compassion and crafted for impact. From nourishing the hungry to empowering young minds and promoting menstrual dignity, each initiative addresses a vital community need. Below are some of our flagship efforts that reflect our mission in action.

Project पोषण

Fighting hunger with daily nutritious meals for underprivileged children and families.

Project Pad House

Ensuring menstrual hygiene through access to sanitary products and awareness.

Project Rakt

A blood donation campaign that has saved countless lives by collecting and distributing blood to those in need.
