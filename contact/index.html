Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to House of Humanity?

You can donate through our website's donation page, by bank transfer, or by sending a check to our address. All donations are tax-deductible, and we provide receipts for your records.

How can I volunteer with your organization?

We welcome volunteers! Please fill out our volunteer application form on the "Get Involved" page or contact us directly. We have opportunities for various skills and time commitments.

Do you provide certificates for donations?

Yes, we provide official donation receipts for all contributions. These can be used for tax deduction purposes according to Indian tax laws.

How is my donation used?

Your donations directly support our humanitarian programs. We maintain transparency with our financial reports available on the website, showing how funds are allocated across different initiatives.
