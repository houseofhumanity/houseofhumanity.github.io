PROJECTS / MAYA

Little Bites, Big Impact

Project Maya is a flagship initiative by House of Humanity Charitable Trust aimed at addressing malnutrition among hospitalized children. Through this program, we've provided healthy and nutritious meals to nearly 100,000 children admitted at SSG Hospital in Baroda.

We believe that proper nutrition is a fundamental right for every child, especially during their recovery period. Our carefully planned meals are designed to meet the specific nutritional needs of growing children while making the hospital experience a little more comfortable.

With the help of our dedicated volunteers and generous donors, we've been able to make a significant difference in the lives of these young patients, contributing to their faster recovery and overall well-being.
