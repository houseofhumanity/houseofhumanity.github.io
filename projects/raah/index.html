PROJECTS / RAAH

Paving the Path to Education

Project Raah is a flagship initiative by House of Humanity Charitable Trust focused on providing essential educational supplies to students from rural schools. We believe that every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their background or location.

Through Project Raah, we distribute school bags, notebooks, water bottles, pencil boxes, and uniforms to children who need them most, ensuring they have the necessary tools to focus on their learning and development.

The project was inaugurated at Mallu village, Waghodia with a beautiful tree plantation ceremony followed by distributing educational kits to 30 children, marking the beginning of our journey to transform rural education.
