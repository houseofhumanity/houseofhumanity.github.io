PROJECTS / RAKT

Blood that heals

Project RAKT is House of Humanity's flagship initiative focused on blood donation and awareness. In Sanskrit, "Rakt" means blood, symbolizing the essence of life that we help circulate to those in need.


Over the past 5 years, we have successfully collected nearly 1000 liters of blood at Blood Bank, contributing to a total of 1000 liters donated across all our campaigns.


Each donation can save up to 3 lives, meaning our collective efforts have potentially helped save thousands of lives across our communities. We organize regular blood donation camps, awareness sessions, and emergency blood arrangements for those in critical need.
