PROJECTS / PAD HOUSE

Breaking Taboos, Building Futures

The Pad House Initiative by House of Humanity aims to break the silence around menstrual hygiene and empower young girls to face this natural process with dignity and confidence.

In many communities, menstruation remains a taboo subject, leading to misinformation, shame, and poor hygiene practices. Our initiative addresses these challenges through comprehensive awareness sessions and distribution of free sanitary products.

Over the past 5 years, we have reached nearly 10,000 school-going girls across multiple regions, providing them with crucial knowledge about menstrual health and hygiene, along with free sanitary pads to ensure they don't miss school during their periods.

Every girl deserves access to proper menstrual hygiene products and education. Through Pad House, we're working to make this basic right a reality for all.
