PROJECTS / पोषण

While one-third of global food goes to waste, over 821 million people still face hunger. To help bridge this gap, we are serving fresh, nutritious meals every day to 500 underprivileged individuals, offering dignity through food where it's needed most. Here are some programs we are running:

Daily Meal Program

Providing nutritious meals to 500 underprivileged people every day across multiple locations.

Food Distribution

Distributing food grains and essential supplies to families in need during crises and emergencies.
